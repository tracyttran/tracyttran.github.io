Hi there, I'm Tracy!

I'm a program manager/maker/computer scientist/artist.

As a Microsoft program manager, I build modern input features such as a PowerPoint presenter coach that promotes inclusive presentations.
As a researcher, I fabricated tactile interfaces for mobile accessibility and simulated the corrosion of nuclear waste canisters.
As an artist and maker, I created a whimsical vending machine that trains AI systems to maternity shirts that respond to inappropriate touching.

I'm intrigued by exploring and challenging connections to each other and our environments.

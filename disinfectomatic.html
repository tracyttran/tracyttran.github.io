An automatic phone disinfecter for COVID times

Built as part of The Garage's "Mystery Maker Box" Challenge, where we were challenged to create something out of a box of mystery, random materials.
Presenting: The Disinfectomatic. During COVID times, we wash and sanitize and disinfect everything. Our hands, our groceries, our hands again. But you know what most people don't disinfect? Their phones. It goes everywhere and gets exposed to everything, and then we go home and wash our hands and pick up our phone again. With the Disinfectomatic, toss in your phone as soon as you get home. An automatic quick spritz and a few wipes later, ding! There you go.
